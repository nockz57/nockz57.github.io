Kwesta's Big Night

Kwesta just turned what was known as SAMAs to a one man show. Well Nasty-C took one but that's not the point. When you talking about SAMA 2017, you talking about kwesta's big night. The homee finally got what he derseved, we all know how long he has been in the industry and he is one of the most consistent guys. He proved he is one of the best when he killed that freestyle on Sway, the one that your favorite rappers choked on. If you listened to Dakar II then you know every award that the homee received was well deserved. Ngud was playing everywhere and it gave a lot of guys a sweet escape to buy and enjoy their beer (750 ml), instead of trying to push swagg with their fancy feminine 440 or 350 ml cans. He walked away with 5 awards that night, including the Best Rap Album, which is an award that matters in every awards show this days. I mean we all that hiphop has took over, hardluck to the other guys. Anyway here is the full list of awards that the homee collected.

  • Samro Highest Airplay of the Year
  • SAMPRA Highest Airplay of the Year
  • Best Rap Album
  • Best Male Artist of the Year
  • Best Album of the Year
